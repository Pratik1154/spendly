Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using Spendly. This includes expense entries with categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions. We also collect account information such as your email address and authentication credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve our expense tracking services. We use your expense information to generate summaries, charts, and insights about your spending patterns. We do not sell or share your personal financial data with advertisers or third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. You retain ownership of all data you enter, and you may export or delete your data at any time through your account settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with external financial institutions or payment processors at this time. All expense entries are manually input by users. We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to addressing your concerns and ensuring your data is handled responsibly.
